# Green Guardian Swap Marketplace Policy

## Welcome to the Swap Marketplace

The Green Guardian Swap Marketplace is a community-driven platform designed to promote sustainability by allowing users to trade unwanted items instead of discarding them. This policy outlines the rules, guidelines, and best practices for participating in our swap community.

---

## 1. General Guidelines

### 1.1 Eligibility
- Only verified Green Guardian users with the "Citizen" role can participate in the Swap Marketplace
- Users must be at least 13 years old to use the platform
- All users must have verified email addresses

### 1.2 Purpose
- The marketplace is intended for trading items you no longer need with others in the community
- All swaps should be conducted in good faith with mutual benefit
- The goal is to reduce waste and promote circular economy principles

1. **Clear Title**: Descriptive and accurate

   - Poor: Significant wear or damage but still usable
4. **Clear Photo**: Show the actual item (not stock photos)
5. **Estimated Value**: Reasonable estimate in PHP (Philippine Pesos)

## 3. Swap Request Process

### 3.1 Making a Swap Request
When requesting a swap:
1. **Review the item carefully**: Read the full description and examine photos
2. **Prepare your offer**: Describe what you're offering in exchange
3. **Provide offer details**:
   - Clear description of your item(s)
   - Estimated value of your offer
   - Upload a photo of your offered item (optional but recommended)
4. **Submit your request**: The item owner will review your offer

### 3.2 Offer Guidelines
Your offer should:
- Be of comparable value to the requested item
- Be in the condition you describe
- Match what you're actually willing and able to provide
- Be made in good faith

### 3.3 Response Time
- Item owners should respond to swap requests within 7 days
- If no response is received after 7 days, you may withdraw your request
- Multiple users can make offers on the same item

---

## 4. Communication and Negotiation

### 4.1 Contact Process
- Once a swap request is accepted, both parties will receive contact information
- All initial communication happens through the platform
- Users may arrange direct contact for swap logistics

### 4.2 Meeting Guidelines
For safety when meeting to swap items:
1. **Choose Public Locations**: Meet in well-lit, populated areas
2. **Bring a Friend**: Consider bringing someone with you
3. **Daytime Meetings**: Prefer meeting during daylight hours
4. **Inspect Before Swapping**: Verify the item matches the description
5. **Trust Your Instincts**: If something feels wrong, cancel the swap
6. **No Pressure**: Don't feel obligated to complete a swap if you're uncomfortable

### 4.3 Alternative Arrangements
- Users may agree to ship items (at their own risk and expense)
- Shipping arrangements are between users only
- Green Guardian is not responsible for shipping issues

---

## 5. Swap Completion

### 5.1 Successful Swaps
When a swap is completed:
1. Both parties should confirm the exchange was successful
2. Items should match the descriptions provided
3. Both parties should update their listings as "swapped"
4. Consider leaving feedback (if system available)

### 5.2 Cancellations
Either party may cancel before items are exchanged:
- Provide notice as soon as possible
- Be respectful and honest about your reasons
- No penalties for legitimate cancellations
- Repeated cancellations may result in account review

---

## 6. Dispute Resolution

### 6.1 Common Issues
If problems arise:
1. **Item not as described**: Contact the other party first
2. **No-show for swap**: Allow reasonable time, then withdraw
3. **Safety concerns**: Report immediately to platform administrators
4. **Scam attempts**: Report suspicious activity

### 6.2 Reporting Process
To report an issue:
1. Document the problem (screenshots, photos, messages)
2. Use the "Report" button on the item or user profile
3. Provide detailed information about the issue
4. Allow 48-72 hours for administrative review

### 6.3 Platform Response
Administrators will:
- Investigate all reports thoroughly
- Contact both parties for their perspective
- Take appropriate action based on findings
- Maintain confidentiality throughout the process

## 7. User Safety and Privacy

### 7.1 Personal Information
- Only share personal contact information when you're comfortable
- Never share financial information (credit cards, bank accounts)
- Be cautious with your full address until you trust the other party
- Your email is visible to facilitate communication

### 7.2 Safety Tips
- Trust your intuition about other users
- Don't accept offers that seem too good to be true
- Verify items in person before completing swaps
- Keep records of all communications
- Report suspicious behavior immediately

### 7.3 Privacy
- Your swap history may be visible to other users
- Personal messages are private between parties
- Administrators may review communications if disputes arise
- Your data is protected per our Privacy Policy
